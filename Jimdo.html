Online portfolio FAQ

Creating an online portfolio is one of the best ways to show off your best work, all in one place. It gives you a hub for potential clients and recruiters to see what you can do on a platform you control.

The best way to start is with a custom domain and high-quality images. Choose a design that complements your work and make sure to optimize for search engines so people can find you.

Include a short bio, professional headshot, and your absolute best work. Don't post everything—be picky and curate your projects to show your versatility and skills.

Visuals are the story for a designer. An online portfolio lets you showcase your personality through photography, modeling shots, or graphic design in a way a paper resume cannot.

Pick a style that fits your brand. Clean and modern designs often work best as they showcase your work without distractions.
